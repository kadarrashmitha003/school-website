Welcome to KGBV(M) Kashipur Minority School

At KGBV Kashipur Minority School, we are committed to providing quality education, fostering holistic development, and empowering students to become responsible citizens with strong academic and extracurricular skills.

Our Vision

Our school envisions a future where every student, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, empowering them to achieve their full potential and contribute to society.

Why Choose KGBV Kashipur?

We offer state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated teaching staff, and a wide range of extracurricular activities that help students develop skills beyond academics. Our approach nurtures creativity, teamwork, and leadership.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our school boasts modern classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, sports facilities, and a spacious library. These facilities create an ideal environment for learning and growth.
