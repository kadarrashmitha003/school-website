Welcome to KGBV Kashipur Minority School

At KGBV Kashipur Minority School, we strive to create an environment where students are encouraged to excel academically, socially, and personally. Our mission is to nurture young minds with the tools, knowledge, and skills necessary to face the challenges of the future.

Hostel Facilities

Our school provides hostel facilities for students from distant areas, ensuring a safe and comfortable stay. The hostels are equipped with modern amenities, 24/7 supervision, and a homely environment. The students are provided with nutritious meals and well-maintained rooms with study tables, beds, and wardrobes.

Food and Dining

Our school provides hygienic and nutritious meals to students. The food is prepared fresh every day with a focus on balanced nutrition to support the students' health and energy levels. The dining hall provides a comfortable space for students to enjoy their meals together.
